DENNIS RITCHIE

Dennis Ritchie was born in 9 september 1941 in Bronxville,New York.He graduated from Summit High School. And then he entered Harvard University with degrees in physics and applied mathematics.He started postgraduated in Harvard. Ritchie chose Phd subject as nature and computation complexity of the program. In 1968,hr completed the Phd.In general he known as "dmr".He entered Bell Laboratory in 1967.He worked on multics,bcpl,altran,b and c programming languages and Unix,plan 9 operating systems in Bell Labs. Dennis M.Ritchie and his friend Ken Thompson created C programming language and Unix operating system.

Ritchie wrote "the c programming language" book with Brian Kernighan in 1978. The book is translated into more than twenty languages and the second edition published the reneval. During the last years of his life,he was caught on prostate cancer and heart disase. He died in 12 october 2011 in Berkeley Heights,New Jersey.
